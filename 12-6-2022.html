Have your kids help make your home more festive with homemade wreaths.

Materials:

  • paper plate
  • ribbon
  • colored tissue/construction paper
  • glue
  • scissors/hole-punch

Instructions:

  • Cut a hole in the center of your plate
  • Tear or cut up pieces of colored paper
  • Glue colored pieces to the plate
  • Add a large ribbon
  • Punch a hote in the top and add a hanging string
  • Display anywhere - even on your child's bedroom door!
