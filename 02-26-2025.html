Roll-A-Story Activity

For Younger Kids

Ask your child to draw a picture on each side of a paper cube—maybe a monster, rocket, castle, pirate ship, magical tree, or mysterious door.

Prep:

  • Cut up a cereal box, tissue box, or thin cardboard and tape or glue it into a cube shape.
  • Or, repurpose a small gift box and wrap it in white paper.
  • Use markers, crayons, or magazine cutouts to decorate each side.

For Older Kids

Challenge them to create their own story prompts by assigning an idea, setting, or object to numbers 1-12. Then, roll one or two dice and combine the numbers to build a unique story!
